Croissant gallery

Croissants are made using a technique called lamination, which layers dough and butter for that signature flaky texture. The traditional French recipe for croissants dates back to the 19th century, blending Austrian influences with French baking techniques. Enjoying a freshly baked croissant is a popular breakfast ritual in France, often paired with coffee for the ultimate morning treat!
